Privacy Notice:

 

The Verbalucce extension (“Verbalucce” or “Application”) is provided by Intel Corporation and is designed for use with GMail. 

 

If you choose to download Verbalucce, it will analyze all of your email communications for congruence in emotional tone and language style , and it provides you with  immediate feedback about the selected email exchange along with the ability to review basic analytics across your email contacts  - for example, you can choose to examine  the types of emotions (excitement, anger, calmness, sadness), and the formality of communication in different relationships.

 

Information Collected:  When installed, Verbalucce collects your email address along with the email addresses of your contacts.  As you use Verbalucce, it records the length of emails, and the number of emails sent from/to you, by contact.  (e.g. 37 email between you and Jane Smith), and the types of words used in order to generate the Application feedback.    

 

Your email content is not retained or stored by Verbalucce.     

 

Information Use and Access:  Email addresses will only be used to sort the Application feedback by contact, and may be used by you as unique keys when you query the database for the specified contact. The information collected is used by Intel to provide and maintain the Application, to provide feedback and analysis to you, and to analyze the functionality, use, and effectiveness of the Application (the “Purpose.”).   The content of email is extracted for analysis by the Application, but it is not stored.    

 

Your information will only be shared with Intel agents, vendors or subcontractors solely as necessary for the Purpose.  In addition, Intel may share technical Application analytics and anonymized Application feedback  (but not email addresses or email content) with third party researchers for the purpose of studies on technology interaction..
